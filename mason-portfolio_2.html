01 —The F&B & Hospitality Pain

How much budget is enough? Which ads actually bring guests?

Hotel guests book via OTA. Restaurant guests walk in from nowhere. How do you know which KOL, which ad actually drives revenue — or just burns budget for reach?

🏨
Hotel
Guests book via OTA — no way to trace which ad or KOL drove the booking. High cancel rate with no clear cause. Top source country? Still guessing.
🍽️
Restaurant
Walk-in guests — unknown origin. Facebook or Google? Which KOL? No real ROAS measurement, every budget decision is gut feel.
📊
Marketing Team
11-step manual KOL management. Excel reports take all day. No right tool — too expensive, too generic, doesn't fit real workflows.
02 —It Can Be Measured

The problem isn't that it can't be done — no one's built it right.

About 3 years ago, building a custom marketing system for F&B & Hospitality required hiring a dedicated engineering team — months of work and serious budget. Small marketing teams simply didn't have that option.

Now it's different. AI compresses that technical gap to weeks. I've proven this by building 3 real platforms for the hospitality industry, with a 4-person marketing team.
